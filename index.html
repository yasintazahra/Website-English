1. Simple Present Tense

Simple Present Tense adalah bentuk tense yang paling umum digunakan. Bentuk ini biasanya digunakan untuk menunjukan fakta, kebiasaan, dan kejadian yang terjadi pada saat ini.

Formula :

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + V1 s/es + Object
  2. (-) Negative: Subject + Do/does + not +V1 + Object
  3. (?) Interrogative: Do / Does + Subject + Verb 1 + Object?

Example :

  1. (+) She reads Harry Potter books every day.
  2. (-) She doesn’t read Harry Potter books every day.
  3. (?) Does she read a Harry Potter book?

2. Present Perfect Tense

Present Perfect Tense menekankan pada hasil. Tense ini menunjukkan aksi yang masih sedang berlangsung atau baru saja selesai.

Formula :

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + Have/Has + V3 + Object
  2. (-) Negative: Subject +Have/Has + Not + V3 + Object
  3. (?) Interrogative: Have/Has + Subject + V3 + Object?

Example :

  1. I think I have seen that movie before.
  2. How many times have you traveled abroad?
  3. Joan has studied two foreign languages.
  4. I’ve been to Bali three times. Have you ever been there?

3. Simple Past Tense

Simple Past Tense digunakan untuk mengungkapkan suatu kejadian yang terjadi di masa lampau. Dalam kalimat Simple Past Tense, kata kerja yang digunakan merupakan bentuk kata kerja kedua.

Formula :

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + V2 + Object
  2. (-) Negative: Subject +Did + Not + V1 + Object
  3. (?) Interrogative: Did + Subject + V1 + Object?

Example :

  1. She joined the English club class at school yesterday.
  2. Ade went to school with his new car this morning.
  3. Last Monday, Joni got in an accident at the office.
  4. Dani bought new laptops last night.
  5. They watched “Eternals”, the newest movie at the cinema yesterday.

4. Past Continuous Tense

Past Continuous Tense digunakan untuk mengungkapkan bahwa suatu aksi sedang terjadi pada waktu tertentu di masa lampau. Biasanya ada dua kejadian di masa lampau dan satu kejadian menginterupsi kejadian yang lain.

Formula :

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + was/were + Ving + Object
  2. (-) Negative: Subject +was/were + Not + Ving + Object
  3. (?) Interrogative: Was/were + Subject + Ving + Object?

Example :

  1. I was having dinner at this time yesterday.
  2. What was she doing at 7 am this morning?
  3. They were making toasts at this time last night.
  4. I was doing physical exercises all day yesterday.
  5. In July she was working as a waitress at a well-known restaurant.

5. Past Perfect Tense

Past Perfect Tense digunakan untuk menunjukkan sebuah kejadian yang terjadi sebelum waktu tertentu di masa lampau dan lebih menekankan pada fakta daripada durasi.

Past Perfect Tense dibentuk dengan auxiliary verb had, dan past participle (verb-3). Had digunakan baik untuk singular maupun plural subject. Sedangkan past participle dibentuk dengan menambahkan –ed, -en, -d, -t, -n, atau -ne pada base form berupa regular verb.

Rumus

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + Had + V3 + Object
  2. (-) Negative: Subject +Had + Not + V3 + Object
  3. (?) Interrogative: Had + Subject + V3 + Object?

Contoh Kalimat

  1. When he came last night, the cake had run out.
  2. The student had gotten a verbal warning before his parents were called.
  3. I had already eaten my breakfast by the time he picked me up.
  4. Prior to the proclamation, Indonesia had been colonized by Japan for 3 years.

6. Past Perfect Continuous Tense

Hampir serupa dengan Past Perfect Tense, Past Perfect Continuous Tense digunakan untuk mengungkapkan suatu aksi yang terjadi di masa lalu dan telah selesai pada suatu titik waktu tertentu di masa lalu. Bedanya, Past Perfect Continuous Tense menekankan pada durasi kejadiannya.

Past Perfect Continuous Tense dibentuk dengan auxiliary verb had dan been serta present participle. Secara umum Past Perfect Continuous Tense hanya terjadi pada aksi berupa dynamic atau action verb, tidak stative verb karena umumnya hanya dynamic verb yang memiliki bentuk continuous.

Rumus

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + Had + been + Ving + Object
  2. (-) Negative: Subject +Had + Not + been + Ving + Object
  3. (?) Interrogative: Had + Subject + been + Ving + Object?

Contoh Kalimat

  1. She was annoyed since she had been waiting for 2 hours at the bus station.
  2. By the time the teacher came, the students had been reading half part of the text.
  3. I had been working with my computer all day, and all I wanted to do was eat some chocolate.

7. Simple Future Tense

Simple Future Tense mengindikasikan suatu aksi di masa yang akan datang dan tidak dapat dipengaruhi. Tense ini dapat juga digunakan untuk pembuatan keputusan di masa depan yang spontan atau asumsi yang berhubungan dengan masa yang akan datang. Will dan be going to merupakan dua indikator umum pada tense ini.

Rumus

Will (dilakukan secara spontan)

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + will + V1 + Object
  2. (-) Negative: Subject +will + not + V1 + Object
  3. (?) Interrogative: Will + Subject + V1+ Object?

Going to (dilakukan secara terencana)

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + tobe (is, am, are) going to + V1 + Object
  2. (-) Negative: Subject + tobe (is, am, are) + not + going to + V1 + Object
  3. (?) Interrogative: tobe (is, am, are) + Subject + going to + V1 + Object?

Contoh Kalimat

  1. They will take a school examination next month.
  2. Budi will take a public speaking class tomorrow.
  3. Peter is going to move to his new house next week.
  4. Dini is going to finish her homework tonight.
  5. I am going to take an English class at Aku Pintar next monday.

8. Future Continuous Tense

Future Continuous Tense menunjukkan aksi yang terjadi pada waktu tertentu di masa yang akan datang dan aksi yang pasti akan terjadi dalam waktu dekat.

Rumus

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + will + be + Ving + Object
  2. (-) Negative: Subject +will + not + be + Ving + Object
  3. (?) Interrogative: Will + Subject + be + Ving + Object?

Contoh Kalimat

  1. Mikaela will be running in a marathon race this Sunday morning.
  2. I will be staying with my sister for a week.
  3. I will not be staying with my sister for a week.
  4. Erika will not be competing against Mikaela in the race when the race starts.

9. Future Perfect Tense

Future Perfect Tense menekankan pada aksi yang sudah berakhir pada waktu tertentu di masa yang akan datang.

Rumus

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + will + have + V3 + Object
  2. (-) Negative: Subject +will + have + not + V3 + Object
  3. (?) Interrogative: Will + Subject + have + V3 + Object?

Contoh Kalimat

  1. Call me at 2 p.m. I’ll have eaten lunch by then.
  2. By next Friday we’ll have moved into our new home!
  3. Let’s wait until 5 p.m. The game will have finished by then.
  4. We’ll have resolved the IT problem by this afternoon.
  5. In May she’ll have been in Australia for ten years.

10. Future Perfect Continuous Tense

Hampir sama dengan semua jenis Perfect Continuous Tense, Future Perfect Continuous Tense merupakan serangkaian aksi yang terjadi sebelum waktu tertentu di masa yang akan datang.

Rumus

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + will + have + been + Ving + Object
  2. (-) Negative: Subject +will + have + not + been + Ving + Object
  3. (?) Interrogative: Will + Subject + have + been + Ving + Object?

Contoh Kalimat

  1. I will have been waiting here for three hours by six o'clock.
  2. By 2015 I will have been living in London for sixteen years.
  3. When I finish this course, I will have been learning English for twenty years.
  4. Next year I will have been working here for four years.

11. Past Future Tense

Past Future Tense pada dasarnya digunakan dalam kalimat yang tidak langsung. Terdapat perubahan bentuk untuk menyesuaikan dengan rangkaian peristiwa yang ada.

Rumus

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + would + bare infinitive + Object
  2. (-) Negative: Subject +would + not + bare infinitive + Object
  3. (?) Interrogative: Would + Subject + bare infinitive + Object?

Contoh Kalimat

  1. My uncle told me that he would come on time.
  2. She promised she would give me a box of banana cake.
  3. They said that they were going to visit Lombok.

12. Past Future Continuous Tense

Past Future Continuous Tense menekankan pada serangkaian atau durasi sebuah aksi terjadi. Sebagai tambahan, Past Future Continuous Tense juga biasanya digunakan pada kalimat tidak langsung.

Rumus

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + would + have been + V-ing.
  2. (-) Negative: Subject + would + not + have been + V- ing.
  3. (?) Interrogative: Would + subject + have been + V-ing?

Contoh Kalimat

  1. I would have been working in leading companies for 5 years.
  2. I would not have been working in leading companies for 5 years.
  3. Would you have been working in leading companies for 5 years?

13. Past Future Perfect Tense

Terdapat tiga waktu dalam Past Future Perfect yang terjadi di masa lalu. Tiga waktu tersebut menunjuk pada waktu yang akan datang, waktu tertentu di masa lalu, tetapi aksi yang terjadi harus sudah selesai sebelum waktu yang akan datang. Past Future Perfect biasanya digunakan pada kalimat tidak langsung.

Rumus

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + should/would + have + V3 + Object
  2. (-) Negative: Subject +should/would + not + have +V3 + Object
  3. (?) Interrogative: Should/ Would + Subject+ V3+ Object?

Contoh Kalimat

  1. If you had saved your jewelry and foreign currency in a safety deposit box, they wouldn’t have gone.
  2. Before taking a leave of absence yesterday, he would complete his work for the client. He would have submitted it two days ago.

14. Past Future Perfect Continuous Tense

Past Future Perfect Continuous Tense merupakan bentuk tense yang cukup sulit. Namun, kamu dapat mempelajarinya dari Past Future Perfect Tense. Bedanya, Past Future Perfect Tense menekankan pada durasi waktu yang sudah berlalu dan hasil.

Rumus

  1. (+) Positive: Subject + would + have + been + Ving + Object
  2. (-) Negative: Subject + would +have + not + been +Ving + Object
  3. (?) Interrogative: Would + Subject+ have+ been+ Ving+ Object?

Contoh Kalimat

  1. I heard that you should have been teaching here for ten years by this July.
  2. I did not know that he would have been working for the past three hours by that time.
  3. Would he have been explaining to the workers if he had made the information clear?

Electronics Product Negotiation

Context: A customer is negotiating with a sales representative in an electronics store for a laptop purchase.

English Version

Customer: Good morning. I’m looking for a reliable laptop for graphic design. I saw the Dell XPS 15 on display.

Sales Rep: Good morning! Yes, the Dell XPS 15 is a great choice for design work. It has a high-resolution screen and powerful specs.

Customer: I like the features, but the price is a bit high. It says $1,500. Is that the final price?

Sales Rep: That’s the listed price, but we do have a special promotion this week—5% off for in-store purchases.

Customer: That brings it down to about $1,425, right? I’m actually planning to buy two units. Can you give me a better deal?

Sales Rep: Hmm… buying two units? Let me talk to my manager for a moment. Alright, we can offer 10% off for two units, which makes it $1,350 each.

Customer: That’s better. What about additional accessories like a wireless mouse or a laptop bag? Can you include those?

Sales Rep: I can include a wireless mouse and a protective sleeve for free.

Customer: Deal. I’ll take two units with those accessories. Do you accept card payments?

Sales Rep: Yes, we accept all major credit cards. I’ll prepare your invoice now.

Customer: Great, thank you.

Sales Rep: Thank you for your purchase!


Terjemahan Bahasa Indonesia

Pelanggan: Selamat pagi. Saya sedang mencari laptop yang andal untuk desain grafis. Saya melihat Dell XPS 15 di etalase.

Penjual: Selamat pagi! Ya, Dell XPS 15 adalah pilihan yang bagus untuk pekerjaan desain. Layarnya beresolusi tinggi dan spesifikasinya kuat.

Pelanggan: Saya suka fiturnya, tapi harganya agak mahal. Tertulis $1.500. Apakah itu harga akhirnya?

Penjual: Itu harga yang tertera, tapi minggu ini kami punya promo khusus—diskon 5% untuk pembelian langsung di toko.

Pelanggan: Itu jadi sekitar $1.425, ya? Sebenarnya saya berencana membeli dua unit. Bisa beri harga lebih baik?

Penjual: Hmm… beli dua unit? Saya bicara dulu dengan manajer. Baik, kami bisa beri diskon 10% untuk dua unit, jadi $1.350 per unit.

Pelanggan: Itu lebih baik. Bagaimana dengan aksesori tambahan seperti mouse nirkabel atau tas laptop? Bisa disertakan?

Penjual: Saya bisa menyertakan mouse nirkabel dan pelindung laptop secara gratis.

Pelanggan: Setuju. Saya ambil dua unit dengan aksesori itu. Apakah menerima pembayaran dengan kartu?

Penjual: Ya, kami menerima semua kartu kredit utama. Saya akan siapkan fakturnya sekarang.

Pelanggan: Bagus, terima kasih.

Penjual: Terima kasih atas pembeliannya!

Formal Conversation Between Secretaries of Company A and Company B

Context: Sekretaris dari dua perusahaan berkoordinasi untuk menjadwalkan rapat resmi.


English Version

Secretary A: Good morning, this is Anna from Company A. May I speak with the secretary of Company B, please?

Secretary B: Good morning, Anna. This is Michael, secretary at Company B. How may I assist you today?

Secretary A: I am contacting you to arrange a meeting between our directors regarding the upcoming project collaboration. Could you kindly inform me of the director’s availability next week?

Secretary B: Certainly. I will check the director’s schedule and get back to you by the end of the day. Do you have preferred dates?

Secretary A: Ideally, we would prefer Wednesday or Thursday afternoon.

Secretary B: Understood. I will prioritize those dates and confirm shortly.

Secretary A: Thank you very much for your assistance, Michael. Please let me know if you require any additional information.

Secretary B: You’re welcome, Anna. I will be in touch soon. Have a good day.

Secretary A: Thank you. You too.


Terjemahan Bahasa Indonesia

Sekretaris A: Selamat pagi, ini Anna dari Perusahaan A. Bolehkah saya berbicara dengan sekretaris Perusahaan B?

Sekretaris B: Selamat pagi, Anna. Saya Michael, sekretaris di Perusahaan B. Ada yang bisa saya bantu?

Sekretaris A: Saya menghubungi Anda untuk mengatur rapat antara direktur kami mengenai kerja sama proyek yang akan datang. Bisa tolong informasikan ketersediaan direktur minggu depan?

Sekretaris B: Tentu. Saya akan memeriksa jadwal direktur dan memberi kabar kepada Anda sebelum akhir hari ini. Apakah ada tanggal yang diutamakan?

Sekretaris A: Idealnya, kami memilih Rabu atau Kamis sore.

Sekretaris B: Dimengerti. Saya akan prioritaskan tanggal tersebut dan segera konfirmasi.

Sekretaris A: Terima kasih banyak atas bantuan Anda, Michael. Tolong beritahu saya jika Anda membutuhkan informasi tambahan.

Sekretaris B: Sama-sama, Anna. Saya akan segera menghubungi Anda kembali. Semoga hari Anda menyenangkan.

Sekretaris A: Terima kasih. Anda juga.
